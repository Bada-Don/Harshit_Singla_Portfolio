CareAllocator is a healthcare platform connecting patients with specialized doctors. Users can search based on preferences, book queue positions via a unique slip system, and an AI optimizes wait times. Future plans include transitioning to a MERN stack for enhanced functionality. Streamlining healthcare, one slip at a time. 🔗GitHub Link🔗

Simon Says Game

Play the classic Simon Says game on harshitsays.netlify.app. Test your memory skills by replicating the sequence of flashed buttons. To contribute, fork the repository, create a branch, make changes, and open a pull request. Inspired by the classic game. Clone the repo, open index.html, and enjoy! 🔗GitHub Link🔗
